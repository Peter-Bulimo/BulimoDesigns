About Peter

Hey there, am Bulimo!

I am pursuing Web Development and UX Design

I derive my motivation in life from the Why and How questions- I want to know why things appear the way they do and how they can be improved to enhance their user’s experience with simplicity at the core. I fancy design work because its user-centered and problem-solving oriented. Interacting with diverse people, problems and products through design enables one to visualize the world through unique lenses, make the impossible possible and ultimately improve the society’s experience of the world.
